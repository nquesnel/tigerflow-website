Overview

TigerFlow is a privacy-focused SEO analyzer extension for Webflow Designer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how our extension operates.

Since no data leaves your browser, your content remains completely private and secure. All analysis is performed using JavaScript running locally on your device.
