Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install TigerFlow?

Install TigerFlow directly from the Webflow Designer Apps panel. Once installed, it will appear in your extensions list and can be launched on any page.

Is my content data safe?

Absolutely! TigerFlow performs all analysis locally in your browser. No data is ever sent to external servers, collected, or stored. Your content remains completely private.

What is the SEO score based on?

The SEO score is calculated based on multiple factors including meta tags, content length, keyword usage, heading structure, readability, and link analysis. Each factor is weighted according to current SEO best practices.

How do I set a focus keyword?

Simply type your target keyword in the "Focus Keyword" field at the top of the TigerFlow panel. The extension will automatically analyze how well your page is optimized for that keyword.

Why isn't my SEO score updating?

Click the refresh button (‚Üª) in the top right of the TigerFlow panel to re-analyze your page. The extension analyzes your page when it loads, but manual refresh ensures you see the latest changes.

What do the different recommendation types mean?

  • Error (Red): Critical issues that should be fixed immediately
  • Warning (Yellow): Important improvements that will help your SEO
  • Info (Blue): Suggestions for optimization
  • Success (Green): Things you're doing well

Can I use TigerFlow on published sites?

TigerFlow is designed specifically for the Webflow Designer environment. It analyzes pages while you're building them, helping you optimize before publishing.

How accurate are the readability scores?

TigerFlow uses the Flesch Reading Ease formula, a widely-accepted standard for measuring text readability. While it's a helpful guide, remember that readability also depends on your target audience.
